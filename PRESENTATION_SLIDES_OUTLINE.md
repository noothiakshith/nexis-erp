## PRESENTATION TIPS

**Body Language:**
- Stand confidently
- Make eye contact
- Use hand gestures for emphasis
- Don't fidget

**Voice:**
- Speak clearly and slowly
- Pause after key points
- Vary tone for emphasis
- Project confidence

**Handling Interruptions:**
- "That's an excellent question. Let me address that..."
- "I'm glad you asked. Here's the specific implementation..."
- "The data shows..." [point to slide]

**If You Don't Know:**
- "That's outside the current scope, but the architecture supports..."
- "That's an interesting point for future research..."
- "Let me show you what is implemented..." [redirect to strength]

**Time Management:**
- Aim for 15-20 minutes
- Save 5-10 minutes for Q&A
- Have backup slides ready
- Can skip slides if running long

---

**Remember: You have the data. You have the proof. You have a system that works. Speak with confidence!**
